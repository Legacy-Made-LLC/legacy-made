# Legacy Made — App Store Screenshot Design Brief

Use this document to create professional App Store and Google Play screenshots for **Legacy Made**, a calm end-of-life planning app for iOS and Android.

---

## About the App

**Legacy Made** is a guided digital platform that helps people organize critical end-of-life information and provide clear direction to their loved ones — in a way that feels calm, human, and manageable.

**Tagline options:**
- "Organize what matters most so those closest are never left guessing."
- "Everything your loved ones would need. All in one place."
- "Give your family clarity. Give yourself peace of mind."

**Tone:** Calm · Human · Respectful · Clear · Unhurried

**What it is NOT:** It is not a legal service, not a will generator, not a memorial platform, and not fear-based.

## App Structure — Four Feature Pillars

The app is organized into four pillars. The MVP focuses on the first two.

### 1. Information Vault (Sage Green — `#8a9785`)
*"The people who need to reach the right people and find the right things."*

Sections inside:
- **Key Contacts** — Who to call first (primary and backup contacts)
- **People** — Important people in your life
- **Finances** — Bank accounts, investments, credit cards
- **Insurance** — Life, health, home, auto policies
- **Documents** — Will, trust, power of attorney, other legal docs
- **Property** — Home, vehicles, storage units
- **Pets** — Pet care instructions and contacts
- **Digital Access** — Email, passwords, devices, social media guidance

### 2. Wishes & Guidance (Soft Lavender — `#B8A9C9`)
*"How you want to be cared for — and what matters most to you."*

Sections inside:
- **Care Preferences** — What matters most, quality of life, advance directive
- **End of Life** — Setting, after death, service, organ donation
- **Personal Values** — Faith, things loved ones should know, guidance for hard situations

### 3. Legacy Messages (Soft Blue — `#A3C4D8`) — *Coming soon*
*"Personal messages and your story, preserved for those you love."*
- Messages to people
- Your story
- Future moments (notes for milestones you won't be there for)

### 4. Family Access (Warm Blush — `#E0B8A8`) — *Coming soon*
*"Share access with the people who need it most."*

## Visual Style for Screenshots

- **Device frame:** Use iPhone 15 Pro (black or natural titanium) or a clean frameless mockup
- **Background behind device:** Use a soft gradient — from `#EEF2EC` (sage tint) to `#F9F8F8` (off-white), or a solid warm off-white
- **Marketing text placement:** Large Libre Baskerville headline above or below the device, DM Sans sub-copy underneath
- **Accent color:** Use sage green `#8a9785` for any highlight shapes, underlines, or background blobs behind the headline
- **No fear-based language** — avoid words like "death," "dying," "tragedy"
- **Preferred language:** "loved ones," "family," "prepared," "clarity," "peace of mind," "what matters"

---

## Do's and Don'ts

| Do | Don't |
|----|-------|
| Use warm, calm language | Use urgent or fear-based messaging |
| Show the app UI clearly inside a device frame | Use busy or cluttered backgrounds |
| Use the sage green as the primary accent | Introduce new colors not in the palette |
| Keep text minimal and readable | Cram too much copy into one screenshot |
| Use Libre Baskerville for headlines | Use a generic system font for headlines |
| Show filled/populated screens with realistic data | Show blank/empty states |
