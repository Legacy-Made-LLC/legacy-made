LEGACY MADE — APP STORE SCREENSHOT DESIGN BRIEF
================================================
Use this to create professional App Store and Google Play screenshots for Legacy Made.


ABOUT THE APP
-------------
Legacy Made is a guided digital platform that helps people organize critical end-of-life
information and provide clear direction to their loved ones — in a way that feels calm,
human, and manageable.

Tagline options:
- "Organize what matters most so those closest are never left guessing."
- "Everything your loved ones would need. All in one place."
- "Give your family clarity. Give yourself peace of mind."

Tone: Calm · Human · Respectful · Clear · Unhurried

What it is NOT: Not a legal service, not a will generator, not a memorial platform,
not fear-based.

APP STRUCTURE — FOUR FEATURE PILLARS
-------------------------------------

1. INFORMATION VAULT (Sage Green — #8a9785)
   "The people who need to reach the right people and find the right things."
   Sections:
   - Key Contacts — Who to call first (primary and backup)
   - People — Important people in your life
   - Finances — Bank accounts, investments, credit cards
   - Insurance — Life, health, home, auto policies
   - Documents — Will, trust, power of attorney
   - Property — Home, vehicles, storage units
   - Pets — Pet care instructions and contacts
   - Digital Access — Email, passwords, devices, social media guidance

2. WISHES & GUIDANCE (Soft Lavender — #B8A9C9)
   "How you want to be cared for — and what matters most to you."
   Sections:
   - Care Preferences — What matters most, quality of life, advance directive
   - End of Life — Setting, after death, service, organ donation
   - Personal Values — Faith, things loved ones should know

3. LEGACY MESSAGES (Soft Blue — #A3C4D8) — Coming soon
   "Personal messages and your story, preserved for those you love."
   Sections: Messages to people · Your story · Future moments

4. FAMILY ACCESS (Warm Blush — #E0B8A8) — Coming soon
   "Share access with the people who need it most."
